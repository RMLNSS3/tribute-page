About

Martin Garrix (born Martijn Gerard Garritsen, 1996) is a world-renowned Dutch DJ, record producer, and electronic music sensation. He rose to global fame at age 17 with his breakout hit “Animals,” which dominated charts worldwide and helped define the modern EDM sound.

Over the years, Garrix has headlined major festivals such as Tomorrowland and Ultra Music Festival, collaborated with top artists like Dua Lipa, Bebe Rexha, David Guetta, and Usher, and founded his own label, STMPD RCRDS.

His passion for music, energetic performances, and dedication to innovation continue to make him one of the most influential figures in the global EDM scene.
